01 / Identity

The mark.

The Fixars mark: a gradient gear cradling a stylized F. The gear represents infrastructure, engineering and the act of fixing; the F monogram gives it strong recall. Use the full horizontal lockup by default; the mark alone for app icons, avatars and favicons; monochrome for print and regulatory surfaces.

Primary / Paper#FAF8F3
App Icon / Dark#0A1628
App Icon / Light#FFFFFF
MonochromePrint / Stamp

Mark · standalone applications

The gear+F mark used without the wordmark — app icons (rounded square, 18% padding), avatars, favicons and watermarks. Monochrome for print and low-fidelity contexts. Never smaller than 24px on screen.

App icon / light
App icon / dark

Clear space & usage

Do

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the width of one gear tooth on all four sides.

Don't

Never place the mark on gradients, photography without a scrim, or brand-adjacent colors.

Don't

Do not rotate, skew, stretch, outline, add shadows or animate the core letterforms.

10 / Principles

How we decide.

When a token, component or pattern has no clear answer, return to these.

01
Trust over style.
Every visual choice must make money, identity or reputation more legible. Never the reverse.
02
Numbers first.
Fixars is a number-dense product. Typography, color, and spacing exist to serve numerals.
03
Offline-aware.
Designs must degrade gracefully on 2G, low-end Android, and USSD fallbacks. No layout depends on webfonts loading.
04
One city, four districts.
Sub-apps flex color and glyph. They never fork type, spacing, buttons, or interaction patterns.
