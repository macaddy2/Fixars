Source DNA
What your sketches share
Across all five originals I see a consistent idea: a gear (infrastructure, engineering, fix) combined with something that ascends or cuts through it — a checkmark, an upward needle, a letterform held in negative space, or a flag-tailed F. That combination is the brand idea. These eight concepts keep the gear-plus-motion DNA and explore different ways to resolve it cleanly.
